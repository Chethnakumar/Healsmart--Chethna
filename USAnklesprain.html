Preventing Ankle Sprains: Expert Tips & Tools

Learn how to strengthen and protect your ankles with effective strategies tailored to your needs.

Common Causes & Risk Factors

  • Engaging in high-impact sports (e.g., basketball, soccer).
  • Previous ankle injuries.
  • Weak or unbalanced muscles.
  • Improper footwear.

Symptoms

  • Pain or tenderness around the ankle.
  • Swelling and bruising.
  • Limited range of motion.
  • Inability to bear weight on the affected foot.

First Aid for Ankle Sprains

In case of an ankle sprain, follow these steps immediately:

  • Rest and elevate the ankle.
  • Apply ice to reduce swelling.
  • Wrap the ankle with a compression bandage.
  • Seek medical attention if necessary.
